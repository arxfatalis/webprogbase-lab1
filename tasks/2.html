Yasuo


Yasuo
The Unforgiven
Skins

LOR

Yasuo, the agile and determined swordsman of Ionia, uses the power of the wind against his enemies. When Yasuo was a proud young man, he was accused of killing his own mentor. He could not prove his innocence and was forced to kill his brother from self-defense. Even when the true killer was found, Yasuo could not forgive himself. He still wanders around his native country, and only the wind guides his blade.
