Qiyana


Qiyana
Emperor of the desert
Skins

LOR

In the heart of the jungle, the city of Ishaokan is hidden. Princess Qiyana is the last in the line of succession, but she makes her way to the throne of Yuntal, sparing no one in her path. She confidently gets rid of her rivals thanks to her incredible ability to control the elements. The earth itself unquestioningly obeys the orders of Qiyana, because she imagines herself the greatest elementalist in the history of Ishaokan and wants to rule not just a city, but an entire empire.
